what is the fastest car in the world?

The fastest car in the world is thebugatti veyron.

The Bugatti Veyron Super Sport is listed by Guinness World Records as the fastest production car in the world at 431 km/h (268 mph). The 1,200-horsepower legend beat out an impressive array of other cars, including its predecessor, the Bugatti Veyron, to claim the title.

The Supersport version of the Veyron is one of the fastest road-legal production cars in the world, with a top speed of 431.072 km/h (267.856 mph). The Veyron Grand Sport Vitesse is the fastest sports car in the world, achieving an average top speed of 408.84 km/h (254.04 mph) in the April 6, 2013 test.

You're all wrong, the Bugatti Veyron isn't the fastest car, and neither is the SSC Ultimate Aero. The new fastest car the world was the Bugatti Veyron Super Sport, which hit a top speed of 268 mph on the Volkswagen test site in Ehra-Lesien. The engine is just enlarged, the turbo and intercooler deliver 1200 hp, and this version sheds some weight as well.
